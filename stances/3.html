#3: How do we pay off our national debt?

Our national debt was caused by the American Revolution. When we fought for our freedom, we accumulated a debt when borrowing funds from France, and through bonds.

Like our previous president Thomas Jefferson, we believed in cutting costs to provide more money towards fixing our debt. We did this by reducing our military, cutting federal jobs, and getting rid of unnecessary government programs. Jefferson did not believe in HEAVY taxes, so he did not tax citizens too much.
